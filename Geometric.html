GEOMETRIC SEQUENCE

DEFINITION

A geometric sequence is different from arithmetic sequence. While arithmetic sequence focus on addition and subtraction, geometric sequence focuses more on multiplication and division.

It always has a constant ratio (r) between consecutive terms.

KEEP IN MIND:

an = nth term

a1 = 1st term

r = common ratio

n = total no. of terms

FORMULAS

GENERAL FORMULA

GEOMETRIC SERIES

Sum of the terms in an geometric sequence. There are two types of geometric series: Convergent and Divergent.

MATHEMATICAL PROBLEM 1

a.) How tall will the Ipil-Ipil (Leucaena leucocephala) tree be on the 5th day?

Use the general formula of the geometric sequence

aₙ = a₁ ⋅ r^(n-1)

Substitute the terms based on the given

a₅ = 2 ⋅ 2^(5-1)

Focus on the exponent first, subtract 5 by 1 which makes it equal to 4

a₅ = 2 ⋅ 2⁴

Then, solve for the exponent (2⁴) which equals 16

a₅ = 2 ⋅ 16

Multiply 2 and 16 and that makes our final answer 32

a₅ = 32

FINAL ANSWER: The tree grew 32 cm on the 5th day.

b.) What is the total height of the Ipil-Ipil (Leucaena leucocephala) tree if we sum up all the heights from day 1 to day 5?

There are two formulas to look for the sum in a geometric series, but we’re just going to use one of them. Don’t worry both of them are equal to the same answer if one is right.

Sₙ = a₁ ∙ (1 - r^n / 1 - r)

Substitute the terms based on the given

S₅ = 2 ∙ (1 - 2^5 / 1 - 2)

Solve for the exponent (2^5) and that equals to 32. Also, make sure to solve in the denominator which is 1 - 2 which equals -1

S₅ = 2 ∙ (1 - 32 / -1)

Subtract 1 to 32 which will equal -31

S₅ = 2 ∙ (-31 /-1)

Divide -31 and -1 and that equals to 31

S₅ = 2 ∙ 31

Multiply 2 and 31 and that makes our final answer 62

S₅ = 62

FINAL ANSWER: The total height of the Ipil-Ipil tree after 5 days is 62cm.

MATHEMATICAL PROBLEM 3

We’re going to use the following formula:

Sₙ = a₁ ∙ (1 - r^n / 1 - r)

Substitute the terms based on the given

S₄ = 3 ∙ (1 - 3^4 / 1 - 3)

Solve for the exponent first which is 3^4 and that equals 81. Also, make sure to solve for what’s in the denominator which is 1-3 and that equals -2

S₄ = 3 ∙ (1 - 81 / -2)

Subtract 1 to 81 which results in it being -80

S₄ = 3 ∙ (-80 /-2)

Divide -80 by -2 and that will equal 40

S₄ = 3 ∙ 40

Multiply 3 by 40 and that makes our final answer to 120

S₄ = 120
