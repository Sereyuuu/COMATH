If the first gust of the whisper were to reach a distance of 500 meters, with each subsequent echo increasing in distance by 40 meters constantly, what would be the sum of the distance of the first ten echoes? an= 10
a1= 500
d = 40

a1= 20
d = 5

a.) How much money will he have saved on the 7th day?
b.) What is the total amount of money saved after 7 days? 		an= 7
a1= 50
d = 3
