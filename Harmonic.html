HARMONIC SEQUENCE

DEFINITION

Sequence of numbers whose reciprocals form an arithmetic sequence. If you take the reciprocal of a harmonic sequence, the resulting values will have a constant difference.

In a nutshell, it's an arithmetic sequence but it's fractions

FORMULAS

GENERAL FORMULA

Similar to the formula of an arithmetic sequence

2 Brenda finally opened his animal shelter after numerous amount of preparations. After a few days after its opening, the adoption rates of the animals are improving. At first, only 1 out of 10 (1/10) animals were adopted each month. Later, the rate improved to 1 out of 15 (1/15) animals, and then to 1 out of 20 (1/20) animals. If this pattern continues in the same way, what will the adoption rate be by the 10th month? n= 10
a1= 10
d = 5
3 Zhayne is filling up his water bottle and his friends’ water bottles in the canteen before classes. He was in a rush because Math class was about to start. In the first minute, he fills 1 whole bottle. In the second minute, he fills 1 (1) out of 4 (1/4) of a bottle. In the third minute, he fills 1 out of 7 (1/7) of a bottle, and so on. How much of the bottle will fill up in the 5th minute? n= 5
a1= 1
d = 3

MATHEMATICAL PROBLEM 1

Transform the fractions into reciprocals (Reciprocal means that you should flip the places of the numerator and denominator):

1/16, 1/23, 1/30, … → 16, 23, 30, …

We’re going to use the formula:

It’s basically like the formula of the arithmetic sequence but it’s fractions

aₙ = 1 / a₁ + (n - 1) d

Substitute the terms based on our given

a₁₂ = 1 / 16 + (12 - 1) 7

Solve what’s inside of the parenthesis which is 12-1 which equals 11

a₁₂ = 1 / 16 + (11) 7

Multiply 11 and 7 together and that equals 77

a₁₂ = 1 / 16 + 77

Combine 16 and 77 and that makes our final answer to 1/93

a₁₂ = 1/93

FINAL ANSWER: In the 12th year, the number of people being infected is 1/93

MATHEMATICAL PROBLEM 2

Transform the fractions into reciprocals (Flip the places of the numerator and denominator):

1/10, 1/15, 1/20, … → 10, 15, 20, …

Using this formula:

aₙ = 1 / a₁ + (n - 1) d

Substitute the terms based on our given

a₁₀ = 1 / 10 + (10 - 1) 5

Subtract 10 by 1 because it’s inside of the parenthesis which equals 9

a₁₀ = 1 / 10 + (9) 5

Multiply 9 by 5 which equals 45

a₁₀ = 1 / 10 + 45

Add 10 and 45 together and that makes our final answer 1/55

a₁₀ = 1/55

FINAL ANSWER: The adoption rate by the 10th month will be 1 55.

Transform the fractions into reciprocals (Flip the places of the numerator and denominator):
