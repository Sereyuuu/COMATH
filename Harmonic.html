HARMONIC SEQUENCE

DEFINITION

Sequence of numbers whose reciprocals form an arithmetic sequence. If you take the reciprocal of a harmonic sequence, the resulting values will have a constant difference.

In a nutshell, it's an arithmetic sequence but it's fractions

KEEP IN MIND:

an = nth term

a1 = 1st term

d = common difference

n = total no. of terms

a1= 16
d = 7

At first, only 1 out of 10 (1/10) animals were adopted each month. Later, the rate improved to 1 out of 15 (1/15) animals, and then to 1 out of 20 (1/20) animals. If this pattern continues in the same way, what will the adoption rate be by the 10th month? n= 10
a1= 10
d = 5
